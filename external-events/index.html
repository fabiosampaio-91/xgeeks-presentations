Before the event · the context

KCD Porto: what the CNCF is, and why it matters.

A quick chain of context, because it is what makes KCD Porto significant for our positioning.

01
The CNCF & Cloud Native
The Cloud Native Computing Foundation is the home of Kubernetes and the cloud native ecosystem. 98% of organizations have adopted cloud native techniques; 82% run Kubernetes in production.
02
Our standing · CNCF Silver Member
synvert xgeeks is a CNCF Silver Member, one of the few Portuguese companies with this status, and organizes the only two official CNCF meetups in Portugal: Cloud Native Lisbon and Cloud Native Porto.
03
What a KCD is
Kubernetes Community Days are the official CNCF community conferences. We run the only one in Portugal and the Iberian Peninsula.
We organize it, we do not just attend
In events like Databricks or Big Data conferences, the group participates, which is valuable. With KCD Porto we create and run the entire experience, with full control, and we are recognized as the organizing entity: the responsible specialists in cloud native and DevOps in Portugal. Owning the event is a different position than attending someone else's.

Recognized beyond our own events

xgeeks holds a seat at the biggest stages.

Our community leadership earns recognition beyond the events we run. Through it, xgeeks holds standing roles at major external conferences, giving the brand presence and network access at essentially zero cost.

JNation · Champion
JNation is the biggest generalist tech conference in Portugal: ~1,500 attendees in Coimbra (Convento São Francisco), covering Java, JavaScript, Cloud, AI/ML, Security and more. The 2026 edition (26-27 May) sold out. xgeeks holds a Champion role, representing and amplifying the event.
SREday & LLMday · Ambassador
International conference series (US, Europe, Asia) on reliability, DevOps and cloud (SREday) and on LLMs and AI (LLMday). They run once a year in Lisbon, hosted at PagerDuty. xgeeks holds an Ambassador role and is helping bring them to Porto too.

The takeaway: xgeeks is recognized by the wider ecosystem. That means presence, credibility and network access on the largest and most international tech stages in Portugal, without owning or funding those events.
