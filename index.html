010 · Channel playbookThe dinner mechanics
THE DINNER PLAYBOOK HOW DEALS GET ENGINEERED
synvert xgeeks011 / 015

The operational heart of the plan

The guest list is the product.

01
Curate
Every seat maps to a target account or strategic relationship. No filler. A narrow theme lifts acceptance: "platform engineering at scale," "AI/GPU on K8s."
02
Seat it like chess
Place each target beside an xgeeks engineer who speaks their language — and a happy current client who provides social proof so we never make our own case.
03
Run it peer-led
One moderator, one topic, no hard selling. A discussion guide with planted prompts surfaces capacity & roadmap pain naturally. Brand hosts, doesn't perform.
04
Capture & follow up
Log attendee, tier, topic, signals, next step → CRM within 24h. Hot contacts hear from us within 48h with a continuation, not a thank-you.

A senior person decides to attend based on who else is in the room, not the food. Curation and seating are the highest-leverage tactics we have.

Peers discovering a need — not seller → buyer

How we introduce the business topic.

Open · relationship

Discover · need
"Where are you most capacity-constrained right now?" — listen for capacity gaps, roadmap pressure, hiring pain, delivery risk.
Bridge · conversation
"That's exactly what our teams pick up — want a 20-minute call with one of our platform leads? Totally exploratory."
